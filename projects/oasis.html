Oasis

The first-person-shooter game we made at The Game Assembly

Summary

For our fps game we took inspiration from Destiny and Borderlands.
We wanted to have character progression by having enemies drop weapons for the player
to pick up. But instead of making a bunch of predetermined weapons we chose to make
them modular. Each type of weapon had a number of components that it was made from,
allowing for more than 7 billion unique weapons.

Details

  • Genre: Top-down hack-n-slash
  • Development time: 9 Weeks, half time
  • Language: C++
  • 3rd party tools:
    • Unity
    • Wwise
  • Team: 11 Members
    • 6 Programmers
    • 2 Level Designers
    • 3 Artists
