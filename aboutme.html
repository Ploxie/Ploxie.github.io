About me

I love programming because it allows me to challenge my problem solving skills in a fun way by coming up with creative solutions and smart designs. I'm always striving to improve myself, as a programmer and as a person.
I've had many different projects during my journey, mostly with gaining knowledge in mind. One of the things I've learned is that a simple and understandable design is very important in a project. So I try to focus on quality over quantity where possible.

My dream job would be somewhere I can improve and learn, preferably in a smaller team not to have too focused tasks. But as long as I can code I'll be happy.
